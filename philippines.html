Filipina Brides

Filipiniana

The "Filipiniana" dress is partly derived from the baro at saya, or “blouse and skirt,” style worn by indigenous women before Spain colonized the Philippines in 1521.

Wearing a bejeweled turban with her classic style wedding gown, this Maranao bride embodies elegance and beauty while proudly showcasing her culture and religion.

Tribal
